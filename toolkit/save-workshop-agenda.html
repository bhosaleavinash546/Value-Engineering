SAVE Workshop Agenda — 3-Day Template

The six-phase job plan, time-boxed. Adjust durations to product complexity; never skip the sequence.

Pre-workshop checklist (2–4 weeks before)

✓ItemOwnerDue
Costed BOM validated by finance (material, process, labour, overhead)
Quality & warranty Pareto for the product
Competitor samples procured for teardown
Cross-functional team confirmed (design, mfg, purchasing, quality, finance, supplier)
Savings target & scope agreed with sponsor

Day 1 · Information + Function Analysis

TimePhase / activityOutput
09:00–09:30Kick-off, scope, targetsShared goal & ground rules
09:30–11:001 · Information: cost walk, Pareto, VoCValidated baseline
11:00–12:30Product & competitor teardown (hands-on)Observations wall
13:30–15:302 · Function identification (verb–noun) & FASTFAST diagram
15:30–17:002 · Function–cost matrix & value indexRanked mismatches

Day 2 · Creative + Evaluation

TimePhase / activityOutput
09:00–12:303 · Creative: brainstorm, 6-3-5, TRIZ, SCAMPER per target function200+ raw ideas, tagged
13:30–15:004 · Screening: go / grow / parkShortlist
15:00–17:004 · Weighted scoring & effort–impact matrixTop 25–40 ideas with champions

Day 3 · Development + Presentation

TimePhase / activityOutput
09:00–12:305 · Development: savings estimate, one-time cost, risk, validation needs per ideaDraft value proposals
13:30–15:00Implementation planning: owners, dates, gatesRoadmap
15:00–16:306 · Presentation to sponsor / decision boardGo / no-go decisions logged
16:30–17:00Wrap: funnel setup, next check-in bookedLive savings funnel
Facilitator rules:
  • Defer judgement in Phase 3 — enforce it ruthlessly.
  • Every idea leaves the room with an owner or it dies in the room.
  • Finance validates all savings figures before the Day-3 presentation.
